Introduction

Welcome to AffinityNG, the "next generation" edition of Affinity (alpha2 release)!

AffinityNG is licensed as open-source software under the Apache License, Version 2.0.

AffinityNG is a universal embedded platform for information processing, control and communication, with a graph database at its heart. pathSQL programs are stored as PINs alongside plain data.

Where To Find Information

For an overview of AffinityNG, please have a look at this presentation. Additional context is available in the FAQ, list of features, and assessment of Affinity's strengths.
